About

Born out of the creative environment of the UdK-TU Master Design & Computation research-based program, 3K is a group of artists merging diverse backgrounds in computer science, media informatics, video, dance, and music. Our interest lies in exploring the fusion of multimedia elements to create immersive experiences that challenge and reveal the power dynamics behind the use of biometric tools. With a shared vision of developing an understanding of how emotions are generated, experienced, and expressed in various contexts, encompassing not only the individual's internal world but also the collective social sphere, we create interactive installations where the viewer is given a key role in the representation.
